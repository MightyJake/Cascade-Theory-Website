Creative With Purpose.

Based in the vibrant Pacific Northwest hub of Bend, Oregon, Cascade Theory is the creative venture of Jake Fischer. I specialize in strategic marketing solutions for Central Oregon's diverse business landscape—from emerging tech startups and established outdoor brands to craft breweries, healthcare networks, and growing real estate companies.

What sets Cascade Theory apart is our deep understanding of the Pacific Northwest market and the unique challenges facing Central Oregon businesses. I believe authentic creative work starts with a single, powerful idea—a core truth about your business—that flows naturally through thoughtful design and strategic storytelling tailored for your local and regional audience.

Why Bend, Oregon?

Bend's unique position as a hub for innovation, outdoor recreation, and entrepreneurship creates distinct marketing opportunities. From supporting BendTECH startups to amplifying craft brewery experiences and showcasing outdoor gear innovations, I understand what resonates with Central Oregon audiences and how to scale that impact regionally and nationally.

My Mission:

"My goal is to produce work that not only resonates with Central Oregon's unique business ecosystem but also drives measurable growth. Every project is crafted intentionally for the Pacific Northwest market, because great ideas deserve to create lasting impact in our community and beyond."
