Case Study

Project 3: Craft Brewery Success

Multi-channel marketing campaign for a Central Oregon craft brewery, resulting in increased taproom visits, expanded regional distribution, and stronger community engagement within the competitive Pacific Northwest beer market.

View Case Study
01

Project Overview

This established Central Oregon craft brewery faced increasing competition in Bend's saturated beer market, with over 30 local breweries vying for consumer attention. Despite producing award-winning beers, they struggled to differentiate their brand and expand beyond their immediate local customer base.

The challenge was to create a cohesive brand story that would resonate with both locals and tourists while building sustainable growth through enhanced taproom experiences and strategic regional distribution partnerships across the Pacific Northwest.

02

Multi-Channel Campaign Strategy

Our comprehensive approach integrated storytelling that celebrated Central Oregon's brewing heritage with modern digital marketing tactics. We developed a campaign that highlighted the brewery's connection to local ingredients, community partnerships, and the unique outdoor lifestyle that defines the region.

Campaign Components:

Brand storytelling with local heritage focus
Enhanced taproom experience design
Social media content featuring brewery life
Regional distribution partnership strategy
Community event marketing integration
Tourism partnership development
03

Exceptional Growth Results

The integrated marketing campaign delivered remarkable results across all key performance indicators. The brewery not only increased local market share but also successfully expanded their footprint throughout the Pacific Northwest, establishing distribution partnerships in major metropolitan markets.

Community Integration & Tourism Impact

Beyond traditional metrics, the campaign strengthened the brewery's role in Central Oregon's tourism ecosystem. New partnerships with local hotels, restaurants, and outdoor activity providers created a network effect that benefits the entire Bend community.

The brewery now serves as an anchor destination in Central Oregon beer tours, with visitors specifically planning trips around taproom visits and special events. This sustainable tourism model has created year-round revenue stability and increased the brewery's contribution to the local economy.
