Project 1: Tech Innovation

A comprehensive digital transformation for a Bend-based technology startup, featuring brand development, responsive web design, and targeted marketing campaigns for the competitive Pacific Northwest tech ecosystem.

View Case Study
01

Project Overview

This Bend-based technology startup needed a complete brand overhaul and sophisticated digital presence to compete effectively in the Pacific Northwest's rapidly evolving tech landscape. The challenge was to establish credibility while maintaining the innovative spirit that drives Central Oregon's entrepreneurial community.

Our team conducted extensive market research into the BendTECH ecosystem, analyzed competitor positioning, and developed a comprehensive strategy that would resonate with both local investors and national technology partners.

02

Strategy & Implementation

The comprehensive solution included a modern brand identity system, mobile-first responsive website, and integrated marketing automation platform. Every element was designed to reflect both the innovative nature of the Central Oregon tech scene and the professional standards expected by enterprise clients.

Key Deliverables:

Complete brand identity and visual guidelines
Responsive web application with advanced UX
Integrated marketing automation system
BendTECH community integration strategy
03

Results & Ongoing Impact

Following the launch of the new brand and digital platform, the startup experienced significant growth in both local market recognition and national business development opportunities. The integrated approach delivered measurable results across all key performance indicators.
