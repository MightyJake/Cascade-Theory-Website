Case Study

Project 2: Outdoor Brand Evolution

Complete brand transformation for a Central Oregon outdoor equipment company, featuring visual identity redesign, e-commerce platform optimization, and targeted marketing campaigns for the Pacific Northwest outdoor community.

View Case Study
01

Project Overview

This established Central Oregon outdoor equipment company faced increasing competition from both national retailers and emerging direct-to-consumer brands. Their existing brand identity and digital presence no longer reflected the quality and innovation of their products, particularly within the discerning Pacific Northwest outdoor community.

The challenge was to modernize their brand while maintaining the authentic connection to Central Oregon's outdoor culture that their loyal customers valued. We needed to balance heritage with innovation, appealing to both longtime enthusiasts and a new generation of outdoor adventurers.

02

Brand Evolution & E-commerce

Our comprehensive approach included a refreshed visual identity that honors Central Oregon's natural landscape, a complete e-commerce platform rebuild with advanced product filtering, and an integrated content marketing strategy featuring local outdoor adventures and gear testing.

Transformation Elements:

Modernized brand identity with outdoor heritage
High-performance e-commerce platform
Content marketing with local adventures
Community-focused social media strategy
Product photography featuring Oregon landscapes
03

Growth & Market Impact

The brand evolution and digital transformation resulted in significant improvements across all business metrics. Most importantly, the company strengthened its position as a trusted partner for Pacific Northwest outdoor enthusiasts while expanding their reach to new markets.

Community Impact & Sustainability

Beyond business metrics, the rebrand strengthened the company's connection to Central Oregon's outdoor community. The new content strategy highlights local trails, conservation efforts, and partnerships with regional environmental organizations, positioning the brand as a steward of the landscapes their customers love to explore.
